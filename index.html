About Remine India

Remine India is a recognized startup committed to preserving the environment by providing safe and sustainable solutions for e-waste and Li battery disposal. Our state-of-the-art recycling facility is equipped with cutting-edge technology and trained professionals, ensuring that the materials are processed safely and efficiently.

Our mission is to create a circular economy for electronic waste and lithium-ion batteries, reducing the dependence on raw materials and minimizing the environmental impact. We strive to promote sustainable development and contribute to a cleaner, healthier planet.

Our Services

  • Collection of e-waste and Li batteries
  • Dismantling, sorting, and processing of materials
  • Compliance with regulatory requirements and international standards
  • Transparency and ethical business practices
  • Hassle-free pickup and transportation services
